Ponnah…

I’m really sorry… truly, deeply, painfully sorry.

I never wanted to become the reason you feel uncomfortable —

not on a call, not in person, not ever.

You should have always felt safe with me…

but instead, my words made you feel judged.

And I hate myself for that.

You are the one person I want to protect, not hurt.

The one person I want to lift up, not make insecure.

The one person whose comfort means more to me than my own feelings.

Every time I said something about your looks, I never meant to make you feel less.

You are already enough… more than enough.

And I should have told you that instead of letting stupid words slip out.

Ponnah… you deserve love that makes you feel beautiful, respected, valued, and seen.

I promise I will learn. I promise I will change.

I promise I will speak with care, with love, with gentleness.

Please forgive me… not because I deserve it, but because I truly want to become a better man for you. ❤️
